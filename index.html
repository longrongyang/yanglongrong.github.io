Biography

Previously, I held the position of a M.S. Student at the Intelligence Vision and Information Processing Lab (IVIP Lab), School of Information and Communication Engineering, University of Electronic Science and Technology of China. Additionally, I had the privilege of spending an enriching six-month period working alongside Prof. Tongliang Liu at The University of Sydney, conducting research on Adversarial Learning.
