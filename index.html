Biography

Previously, I held the position of a M.S. Student at the IVIP lab, School of Information and Communication Engineering, UESTC. Additionally, I had the privilege of spending an enriching six-month period working alongside Prof. Tongliang Liu at The University of Sydney, conducting research on Adversarial Learning. Currently, I am in my first year as a Ph.D. student at the Institute of Artificial Intelligence, Zhejiang University. My primary objective is to leverage artificial intelligence research to release productive capacities. More specifically, my research focuses on Noisy Label Learning, Few-shot Learning, Semi-supervised Learning, Self-supervised Learning, and Incremental Learning.
