Biography

Previously, I held the position of a M.S. Student at the Intelligence Vision and Information Processing Lab (IVIP Lab), School of Information and Communication Engineering, University of Electronic Science and Technology of China. Additionally, I had the privilege of spending an enriching six-month period working alongside Prof. Tongliang Liu at The University of Sydney, conducting research on Adversarial Learning. Currently, I am in my third year as a Ph.D. student at the Institute of Artificial Intelligence, Zhejiang University. My primary objective is to leverage artificial intelligence research to release productive capacities. In the past, I focused on Object Detection or Instance Segmentation under Limited Label Settings. Recently, I mainly focus on designing Efficient Training and Inference Algorithms, especially for Large Vision-Language Models.
