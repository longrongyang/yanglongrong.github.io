Biography

I am currently a 3rd-year M.S. Student at IVIPC lab, School of Information and Communication Engineering, UESTC. My aim is to make deep learning models get rid of the excessive reliance to labeled data. Specifically, my research focuses on Noisy Label Learning, Few-shot Learning, Semi-supervised Learning, Self-supervised Learning and Adversarial Training.
